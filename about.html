About

Miguel de Laveaga is a writer/director and editor based in New York City.

He has shot and edited for brands like the PUBLIC Hotel, Anne Klein, Governor’s Ball, Daybreaker, and Maui Jim.

He has directed music videos for artists Cherin, Adie, christ! and Giulia.

His short film “A New Covenant” is currently making its way on the festival circuit. He has worked on Documentaries spanning Guatemala, Lebanon, and Sub-Saharan Africa, as well as right here in nyc.
